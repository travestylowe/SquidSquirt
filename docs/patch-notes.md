# SquidSquirt Patch Notes v2.0 — "The Squid Pro Quo Update"

**We're not squidding around — this is our biggest update yet.**

## New Features

**Anonymous Leaderboard** — Finally, a way to prove you're the most dedicated squid squeezer on the internet. See the top 10 players, check your rank, and pick a name like "Feral Ink Goblin #4271" (or keep it — honestly, that's a *fin*-tastic name). Your existing score uploads automatically — no need to re-squirt. We won't ask for your email. The squid respects your privacy more than most apps respect your time. Names are unique — first come, first *squid*.

**Recovery Codes** — New phone? New browser? Lost your cookies to a rogue sea creature? No problem. Your account now has a recovery code — a string of ocean-themed words that encodes your identity. Show it, screenshot it, tattoo it on your arm. Enter it on any device to reclaim your name, rank, and *ink*-redible squirt count. No accounts, no passwords, no *octo*-factor authentication required.

**In-Game Feedback Form** — Got something to say? Bug report? Feature idea? Just want to say hi to a cephalopod? Hit the new chat bubble button. We *ink*-ourage all feedback. The squid reads every single one. (The squid cannot read.)

**Submission Persistence** — Remember all those heartfelt compliments and terrible puns you typed into the payment modals? They used to vanish into the void. Not anymore — every compliment, confession, and pun is now saved. The squid is building a collection. A *tentacle*-ular archive, if you will.

**Round-Robin Payments** — Tired of always having to dance for the same unlock? The payment system now cycles through all 10 types before repeating. You'll experience the full range of indignity before any repeats. *Ink*-redible variety.

**Bow & Arrow Confetti** — New confetti shape unlocks at 500 squirts, because nothing says "I squeeze digital squids" like tiny arrows raining from the sky. Right between Anchors and Tentacles — filling the gap like ink fills water.

**Immersive Landscape Mode** — Turn your phone sideways and the squid takes over. Counter shrinks to a floating pill, controls become tiny corner icons, and the global bar *squid*-addles out of the way. Pure CSS, zero JS — we're not *fishing* for complexity.

## Visual Upgrades

**Wizard Hat Redesign** — The old wizard hat was three circles and a triangle. The new one is a *masterpiece*: storybook brim, curved cone, golden buckle, crescent moon, shooting star with trailing streaks, and a pulsing purple glow. It's the hat the squid de-*serves*.

**Ink Vibrancy Fix** — Colors were muddier than the bottom of the Mariana Trench. We cranked saturation from 50-65% up to 70-85% and lightness from 14-28% up to 30-45%. Rainbow ink went from "is that brown?" to actual rainbow. The difference is *un-be-reef-able*.

**SVG Clipping Fix** — Tall hats were getting their tips chopped off by the SVG viewBox. One `overflow: visible` later, and hats are free. No more *clipping* the wizard's dreams.

**Weird Names** — Auto-generated leaderboard names are now deeply unhinged. You might be "Suspiciously Moist Barnacle #7832" or "Eldritch Suction Cup #2041" or "Boneless Deep Sea Cryptid #5519". 400 possible combinations, zero dignity. You're welcome.

## Bug Fixes

**Rapid-Fire Easter Egg Actually Works Now** — Due to an off-by-one error, you needed 11 rapid squirts instead of 10 to trigger the water droplets. The 10th squirt was fashionably late to its own check. Fixed — 10 means 10. We're not *kraken* jokes about this one.

**Midnight Easter Egg Window Widened** — Previously required playing at *exactly* midnight in your local timezone. Now triggers within 30 seconds of midnight PST. Because even squids deserve a little *latitude*.
